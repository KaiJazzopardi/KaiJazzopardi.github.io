About John

John Buhagiar is a passionate Maltese photographer, capturing life's essence through his lens. Raised in Malta, he explores the beauty of his homeland, from vibrant sunsets to cultural events. Beyond the camera, John actively engages in the local photography community, contributing to exhibitions and enriching Malta's cultural scene. Join him on a visual journey that freezes time and tells unique stories through the art of photography.
